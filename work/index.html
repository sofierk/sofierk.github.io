AWARDS

Leadership Award
University of California, Berkeley
2014-2015
The Leadership Award is one of the most prestigious merit-based scholarships at the University of California, Berkeley. It is awarded to Cal student leaders who have demonstrated outstanding, innovative, and motivational leadership in academic, work, and/or community environments.

Eleanor Roosevelt Policy Initiative Ambassador
Roosevelt Institute
September 2014
The Roosevelt Institute awarded seven highly competitive ambassadorships to students who wrote longform essays presenting forward-thinking, creative and compelling visions for strengthening and mobilizing the global gender equality movement. The ambassadors were given living and travel accommodations to the invite-only Women and Girls Rising conference in New York City. The conference was attended by Secretary of State Hillary Rodham Clinton, feminist activist Gloria Steinem, and numerous high-level U.N. and NGO officials.
